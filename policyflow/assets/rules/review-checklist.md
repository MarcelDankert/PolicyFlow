# Review Checklist

- Does the change stay within scope and non-goals?
- Is the declared risk level correct?
- Do required reviews match `rules/risk-review-matrix.md`?
- Are protected areas guarded correctly?
- Is confidence credible for the available evidence?
- Were required escalations triggered?
- Are documentation and workflow references aligned?
- Are validation steps appropriate for the change type?
- Are unresolved risks and assumptions stated clearly?
